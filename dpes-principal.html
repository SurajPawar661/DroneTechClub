Principal's Message

Welcome to Dhole Patil College of Engineering!

Dhole Patil College of Engineering has its roots lying deep in the quality education practices that prepare each student with a broad outlook for the futuristic world. We are dedicated to delivering education that satisfies the requirements of today’s world. Today, education is not restricted to course textbooks and practical implementations but is a step ahead. Education is an achievement of skills and knowledge, building character and refining the employability of our young talent - the future leaders. It has become imperative to inculcate additional skills in students to help them succeed in their professional careers and as good human beings.

We encourage and inspire our students to excel in academics and also get involved in co-curricular activities to take up challenges, improve interpersonal skills and personality, become self-dependent, and, above all, enjoy a fulfilling time while learning.

At DPCOE, we make conscious efforts not only to educate students but also to instill integrity, honesty, values, and the ability to work hard. Our vision and mission are to help our students understand their dreams and send them forth into the world as confident and successful entrepreneurs following their dreams.

DPCOE welcomes you to become part of an incredible journey where we encourage you to spread your wings, soar as you follow your dreams, and turn them into reality.

Because we believe in “You dream, we educate…”

With warm wishes,

Principal,
Prof. Dr. Omprakash Rajankar
