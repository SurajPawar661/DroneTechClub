Principal's Message

Welcome to Dhole Patil College of Engineering!

We have been in the field of education for the past decade. In this last decade I have had the dream of putting together an authentic, vibrant and reliable institute operating in a region surrounded by the Pune IT hub. I experience a feeling of great satisfaction to be a part of DPCOE; whose belief is the creation of an educational hub providing opportunities to the talented students from Maharashtra, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Bihar, Jharkhand, West Bengal and overseas too.

We started our college with the vision of providing quality education to rural and urban youths. Well-designed infrastructure, world class engineering laboratories, focus on quality education and industry interaction, boosting academic performances through institute scholarships, keen interest in research, investing in faculty development and continuous improvements are the hallmarks of engineering at our institute. At DPCOE, we provide a conducive environment for young engineers where they can be creative, learn to solve problems and express themselves.
