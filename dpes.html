About Dhole Patil Education Society (DPES)

Dhole Patil Education Society (DPES) is a renowned educational institution based in Pune, India. Established with a vision to provide quality education across various disciplines, DPES has been instrumental in shaping the future of students through its commitment to excellence.

DPES offers a diverse range of educational programs, including engineering, management, pharmacy, and more. The society emphasizes holistic development, industry-relevant skills, and ethical values to prepare students for global challenges and leadership roles.
